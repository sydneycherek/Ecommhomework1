Quick Tip

If you want a really moist cake and don't plan on eating this right after you bake it here is a trick! Right after your cake comes out of the oven, immediately flip it out of the pan and wrap it in plastic wrap. This will hold moisture in until the cake is ready to be frosted and eaten.
Step 1 Step 2 Step 3

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.

Step 2

In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

Step 3

Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
